About Us
Est. 2024

Preserving the Soul of Bengal

Somagom was born from a simple realization: Bangladesh’s most precious treasures—its GI-certified products—were often lost in a sea of mass-produced imitations. We built this platform to bring the authentic "Soil of the Land" directly to your doorstep.

Our Mission

In a world of fast fashion and instant food, the patience of a Jamdani weaver or the precision of a Bogura Doi maker is becoming rare. **Somagom** acts as a bridge between these legendary artisans and a global audience that values quality, history, and ethics.

  • 100% GI Certified Authenticity
  • Direct-from-Source Procurement
  • Equitable Fair-Trade Pricing
  • Cultural Preservation

The Soil

Geographical Indication (GI) isn't just a tag; it's proof that the environment of a specific region gives a product its unique character.

The Skill

Our products are made using techniques passed down through generations, often requiring weeks or months of manual labor.

The Shield

By buying from Somagom, you shield traditional industries from being wiped out by industrial machine-made copies.

A Note from the Collective

"We didn't just want to build an e-commerce store. We wanted to build a digital museum where you could actually own the exhibits. Whether it's the silver shine of a Padma Ilish or the intricate thread-work of a Nakshi Kantha, these items are the fingerprints of our national identity."

— The Somagom Team
